The Game Has Changed

The days of playing random numbers, your favorite numbers, or numbers from a dream are a thing of the past! With the help of our Pick3 Lottery app, you will improve your changes of winning the Pick3 Lottery! Its fun and easy to use! Give it a try TODAY!

Key Features

Generate Picks

With a previously drawn Pick3 Lottery number and our Pick3 Lottery algorithm, we can generate a list of Pick3 Lottery numbers; which you can then manually play at your local Lottery sales agent!

View Winning Picks

Get live Pick 3 Lottery drawing results at your finger tips!
The app will notify you if one of the generated numbers is a winner via:
  • Color System - Green = Winner
  • Color System - Yellow = Generated
  • Color System - Black = Lost
  • Alert - For generated winners

View Past Picks

See a history of the generated numbers from the past week by simply swiping right!

How To Use

1. Draw Date Card

The Draw Date Card show all the draw times for the current date. Each Draw Time Card has color indicators that indicate the state of your generated numbers for a specific draw time.

2. Select Previously Drawn Number

In order to generate a list of numbers you need a previously drawn number by selecting a:

  • number from today or yesterday
  • draw time

The previously drawn number is displayed at the top of Draw Date Card.

Click the Continue button to proceed to the next step in the generate pick process..

3. Generate Picks

In order to select the date and time for the new picks select the:

  • draw date - either today or tomorrow
  • draw time

The previously drawn number is displayed at the top of Draw Date Card.

Click the Generate button to generate a number list.

3. Write Them In!

After you've generated picks, you will be presented with a generated number list; that you can choose to play at your local Pick3 Lottery sales agent!
