Requirements:

Chuck's Pick 3 is a companion app dedicated to helping you generate smart numbers to play in your state's Pick 3 lotto game. You will need the following in order to play:

1. You will need to download Chuck's Pick 3 from the appropriate App Store onto your phone.



2. Gather a Pick 3 lotto ticket from your state.

3. Now with both Chuck's Pick 3 and your Pick 3 lotto ticket at hand you can begin generating and writing down your picks.
